# Tween Style Guide

- Graphic, unsophisticated text
- Graphic cartoon art; not 3d
- Colors:
  - Cerulean / baby blue, yellow / lime yellow, light sap green
  - Off primary colors
  - No all pink, princess colors. They hate that.
  - No all primary schemes (little kid colors).
  - Black is OK, but avoid black and red dark themes. They're not teenagers.
  - Actual Palette
    - BG Light: Light Green hsl(160, 100, 90)
    - BG Dark: Muted Dark Blue hsl(190, 40, 40)
    - Splash: ?
    - Text:
      - light honeydew hsl 120 100 97
      - grey
