Learn the United States capitals!

Need a fast way to learn the state capitals? Want to focus on just the capitals, without getting distracted by lots of trivia? USA Capitals has you covered.

Study how you want!

  • Study all the states or filter by region.
  • Study in list mode or one state at a time.
  • Reverse the state and capital.
  • Hide, show, or "hint" (show only the first letter of) the answer.
  • Tap on the item to hide, show, or hint that item only.

Best of all this app is free and without ads!

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2019

What Information Do We Collect?

We are strong proponents of personal privacy and believe in the golden rule (do unto others as they would do unto you). As such, we don't collect, share or sell your personal information. If you contact us we will have your email address. Apple collects anonymous usage stats, such as number of downloads and information about crashes.

What Do We Do With The Information?

Anonymous usage stats may be used to improve the quality of the app. If you contact us, we will only use your email for purposes of responding to you.

How Can You Delete The Information?

Anonymous usage stats are provided by Apple, and as such we don't have a way to delete. If you have contacted us, send us an email and we'd be happy to delete any personal information you have given us.
